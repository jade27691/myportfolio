About Me

Hi, I'm Jade, I'm 29 years old and I have a passion for all things design. From a young age I have always been interested in computers and taught myself how to use Adobe Photoshop and various other design softwares. This led me to studying Multimedia Technology at University of Chester where I obtained a first class honours degree and developed my graphic design skills and learned new skills such as web development. I have further broadened my experience working currently for the past five years as a Graphic Designer designing adverts, handling social media accounts and updating websites built on WordPress. In the last year I have taken various online courses in web development (HTML, CSS, Javascript & Jquery) and graphic design (Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator & Indesign) to improve my skills and learn new ones.

In my spare time when I'm not glued to my computer screen I enjoy watching tv, hanging out with friends and taking my cute cavachon for a walk. Check out some of my works below.
